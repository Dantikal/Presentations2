About Me

Hi, my name is Sultan! I'm 17 years old, and I was born and raised in sunny Bishkek—a city where the mountains meet the energy of a big city. Since childhood, I've been very active and have tried many sports, from football and wrestling to track and field. Sports taught me discipline, determination, and how to work as a team.

When it comes to simple pleasures, my color is confident and calm blue. My favorite dish is fragrant pilaf seasoned with cumin, which reminds me of home. I don't watch movies often, preferring to live my own stories rather than watch others'. However, my headphones are often playing music—the energetic tracks of LUCAAFROS and Elbrus set the rhythm for my days. In the gaming world, my unconditional choice is CS:GO, where tactics, reflexes, and team spirit are key.

But my main hobby, my true passion, is cars. I don't just like them; I adore them. When I get behind the wheel, everything else ceases to exist. I feel the adrenaline rush through my veins, an incomparable sense of drive, and a feeling of complete freedom. It's not just a way to get from point A to point B—it's art, technology, and emotion all in one. Understanding a car, its character and capabilities—that's what excites me. It's a hobby I'm sure will stay with me forever.

I even have my own superpower—I'm a master at avoiding getting a "telling-off" from my dad! Probably the result of the maneuvering skills I've acquired, not just on the road.

Recently, as soon as I was old enough, I got my long-awaited driver's license. Now I enjoy exploring city routes, and the respect from my friends when we drive somewhere together is a nice bonus.

I believe in constant growth. Right now, I'm actively studying everything related to cars—from how engines work to tuning. At the same time, I'm learning English and Arabic to open up new horizons and cultures for myself. And, like any modern guy, I'm into gadgets—phones for me aren't just devices, but interesting pieces of technology I want to understand inside and out.

So, that's me—Sultan: energetic, driven, and hopelessly in love with the world of speed and technology. Everything is just beginning!
